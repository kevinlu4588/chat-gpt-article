Explore The Stars

The night sky has captivated the human imagination for thousands of years, and one of its most enduring features are the constellations. Throughout history, these patterns of stars have been named by countless cultures, each with their own myths and legends. Today, we can still gaze and marvel at the same stars that captivated our ancestors. In this interactive piece, we invite you to explore some constellations through NASA’s lens by moving the ‘telescope focus’ around to see different stars within starcharts generated using AstronomyAPI.

The constellation starmaps are generated using with your location in mind, but you can also view these constellations as seen from locations across U.S.
